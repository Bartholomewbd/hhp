Toddler Time

We are excited to announce we are expanding our program to toddlers for kids ages 2 and up. The goal of this program is to get our Toddler's socially and emotionally ready to enter our academic Preschool Program. Toddlers will focus on learning through play with a hands-on approach. The toddler program will be separate from our preschool program. Toddler's will have their own scheduled time outside and separate indoor play space when weather does not allow us to go outside.

For this program we do accept children who are not potty trained as assisting with potty training is something we provide. Our class size is small to help give individualized attention to our littlest learners. The current ratio is 6 children : 2 adults.
We have very limited enrollment spots for summer so sign up today!
